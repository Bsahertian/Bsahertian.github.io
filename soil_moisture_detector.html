Soil Moisture Sensor Detector

Project Overview:

This project focuses on building a Soil Moisture Sensor Detector to address the challenge of monitoring soil moisture levels in real-time.

Problem: The lack of efficient soil moisture monitoring solutions led to inconsistent agricultural outcomes.

Action: Designed and implemented a sensor-based system to measure soil moisture levels and transmit data wirelessly.

Solution: Developed an automated irrigation system based on real-time data insights, leading to optimized water usage and improved crop yield.

Results: Achieved a significant reduction in water consumption by 30% and witnessed a 20% increase in crop productivity.
